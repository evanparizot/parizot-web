About me

I am currently working as a Software Developer at Quickenloans. I primarily do fullstack development for web applications with Angular and .Net Core. In addition to web applications, I have experience with AWS and GCP, as well as constructing and managing devops pipelines. Outside of work I like to travel, do woodworking, and play ultimate frisbee quite a bit. Thanks for checking out my site and feel free to reach out!
